Leash Training

Description

Training your dog to walk on a leash is essential for outdoor activities. Use positive reinforcement and short training sessions for the best results.

Steps

1. Start indoors with treats.
2. Introduce the leash gradually.
3. Practice walking in small, distraction-free areas.
4. Reward your dog for calm behavior.
5. Gradually move outdoors and increase the duration.
